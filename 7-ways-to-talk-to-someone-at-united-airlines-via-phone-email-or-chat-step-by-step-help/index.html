7 Ways to Talk to Someone at United Airlines via Phone, Email, or Chat: Step-by-Step Help

If you're facing issues with flight bookings, cancellations, baggage, or just need general assistance from United Airlines, knowing how to talk to a real person can save you time and stress. United Airlines offers several reliable ways to connect with their customer support team. You can always reach them at 1-855-742-0149 for fast support.

  1. United Airlines Customer Service Number (U.S.)
    Call the main customer service line at 1-855-742-0149. Press “0” or say "agent" to bypass the automated system and reach a live person.
  2. International Support Numbers
    United Airlines provides separate customer service numbers for different regions. For international travelers, find your local number or dial 1-855-742-0149 and request to be transferred to the correct department.
  3. MileagePlus Customer Support
    MileagePlus members receive dedicated support. Call 1-855-742-0149, then provide your MileagePlus number for priority service.
  4. United Airlines Mobile App Support
    Use the United Airlines app to navigate to the Help section and initiate a live chat or call request. For urgent issues, still call 1-855-742-0149 directly.
  5. Live Chat via Website
    Go to United.com, scroll to the bottom, and use the live chat feature. If chat isn't responsive, switch to a phone call at 1-855-742-0149.
  6. Chat Support via the App
    The mobile app offers a live chat interface. Launch the chat feature from within the app’s contact menu or call 1-855-742-0149 for a faster connection.
  7. Text Messaging Support
    Text "HELP" to 26266 for SMS support. If your issue requires a deeper conversation, escalate it by calling 1-855-742-0149.
  8. Reach Out via Email
    Send emails for non-urgent matters to customer.relations@united.com. If your issue is time-sensitive, dial 1-855-742-0149.
  9. Use the Feedback Form
    Use the feedback form on United.com/customercare for complaints or compliments. For immediate resolution, call 1-855-742-0149.
  10. Twitter Support (@United)

  11. Facebook Messenger

  12. Instagram Support via DMs

  13. Visit a United Airlines Airport Counter
    For ticketing or boarding issues at the airport, go directly to the United counter. You can also call 1-855-742-0149 to inform them in advance.
  14. United Club Lounges

  15. Contact via WhatsApp (Limited)

  20. Group and Corporate Travel
